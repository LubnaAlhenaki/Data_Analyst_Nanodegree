Introduction¶

The main purpose of this notebook is to explore the Stat Computing Orgnization flight data to answer several questions as mentioned in the coming sections.

The data is obtained from RITA. Also, this project was completed as part of Udacity's Data Analyst Nanodegree certification.

What is the structure of your dataset?

The dataset has 5202096 flight arrival and departure details for all commercial flights within the USA with 29 features. Indeed, The data is separated by year,so for simple exploration and due to the lake of my computer processor that need long time to run large dataset, therefore the data is selected for 1993 only.

I tried to take the 1993 year. It has 5070501 flight details and 29 features. But unfortunately, the dataset has many null values.

The variables in the dataset contains information about flights' origin, departure, date and time, duration, cancellation status, delay (and cause of delay), etc. The variables description is available in details here

Moreover, a flight is considered delayed when its arrival delay is longer than 15 minutes.

What is/are the main feature(s) of interest in your dataset?

In my opinion, there are many features I'm interested in such as canceled and delayed flights that enable me to answer several questions like how canceled and delayed flights are distributed, what causes flights to be canceled or delayed, etc.

What features in the dataset do you think will help support your investigation into your feature(s) of interest?

I think The ArrDelay, DepDelay, Cancelled, CarrierDelay,WeatherDelay,NASDelay,SecurityDelay, LateAircraftDelay will be a useful for the analysis and answer the questions.
